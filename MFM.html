The MFM (Master of Fashion Management) is a postgraduate program focused on the business side of the fashion industry. It combines the creative elements of fashion with business management concepts such as marketing, branding, retail strategy, and supply chain management. The program covers essential areas like fashion trends, merchandising, consumer behavior, and fashion entrepreneurship, preparing students to take leadership roles in the global fashion sector.
