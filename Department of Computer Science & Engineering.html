Department of Computer Science & Engineering


The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is dedicated to fostering innovation, research, and excellence in computing and technology. Our mission is to equip students with strong theoretical foundations, practical skills, and problem-solving abilities to thrive in the ever-evolving digital landscape. Established with a vision to produce world-class engineers, the department offers cutting-edge curricula, state-of-the-art laboratories, and industry collaborations to prepare students for future challenges in software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and more.

Programs Offered :

We offer a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs designed to meet industry demands and global standards:

  • B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering

  • B.Tech CSE - Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

  • B.Tech CSE- Cyber Security

  • M.Tech Computer Science & Engineering

  • M.Tech CSE - Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

  • Diploma Computer Science & Engineering

  • Ph.D. Computer Science & Engineering
